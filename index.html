Problem Statement

People in MENA region are interested in improving their mental health, despite the stigma. The main problem is they don’t know where to start learning about improving their mental health and seeking professional help is also difficult for being expensive and inaccessible. We aim to aid in making a safe space in need of a secure and understanding community, and provide mental and physical health guidance and professional help.

Frequently Asked Questions

After answering a few brief questions, you will be matched with a group of people and a professional therapist that’s right for you. Then you’ll select the time and day that works best for you. Once you sign up for Healthia, your first meeting will take place within a week. You will meet once a week online for a 60-minute live session. Your Circle will consist of no more than 10 other people, all of them coping with a similar struggle. All participants will be new to the group, just like you.
